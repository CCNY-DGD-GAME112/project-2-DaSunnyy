---GOOMBA MANIA

Hello! It's-a-me, Mario! My good buddy has asked me to help him out with one of his game design college projects. So here I am, jumping into action!

Seems like the goal of this trial is to get past all the Goombas and reach the star at the end, being guarded by the Boss Goomba.

No problemo! If I'm ever in a bind, I'm sure there are some helpful power-ups lying around here for me to use. And if there are any gaps too big for even me to jump over, I can use those Jump Panels to give me a boost!

---MARIO's STATS---
MAX HP: 8
ATTACK POWER: 1

---CONTROLS---
WASD to MOVE
SPACEBAR to JUMP (Jump on a Goombas' head to stomp them)
LEFT MOUSE CLICK or K to ATTACK
LEFT SHIFT or J to RUN
RIGHT MOUSE CLICK or L to FIREBALL (Need Fire Flower First)

---MOUSE CAMERA---
The Camera can be moved around with the Mouse. When you grab a Fire Flower, you can use the Camera to aim at the Goombas.

---POWER UPS---
HEART: Restores 3 HP
SUPER MUSHROOM: Grow in size with double Attack Power
FIRE FLOWER: Shoot out Fireballs
